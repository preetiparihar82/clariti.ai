Born from Frustration
Built for Impact

Years ago, our founders sat in a boardroom watching executives argue over whose spreadsheet was correct. Sales had one number, Marketing had another, and Finance had a third.

Despite investing millions in modern data warehouses and BI tools, the actual "decision-making" part of the business was broken. Data was siloed, SQL queries were bottlenecked, and dashboards required interpretation.

We realized the problem wasn't a lack of data; it was a lack of unified semantics and clear narrative context.

That's why we built Clariti. Not just another visualization tool, but a semantic engine that sits on top of your data stack, unifies your metrics, and automatically generates the answers leaders actually need.
